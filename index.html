Welcome to Love Tester, where you enter the fun and frivolous world of romantic searches. It is based on the idea that with the help of a simple system, players can determine the level of reconciliation between two people. At first glance, the plot of the game seems simple and easy, but as you delve into the process, interesting and unexpected elements are revealed.

Plot

The game begins with the main character, a young man, deciding to test himself in the world of dating. He has always wondered how compatible he is with the opposite sex. One day, he finds an old and mysterious device for checking compatibility – Love Tester.

It seems to know more about the brightest feeling than any other gadget or application. According to legend, it was created a long time ago, in the days when love was considered a magical force capable of overcoming any obstacles. According to rumors, it was invented by alchemists trying to create the perfect formula for finding a soulmate.

Rules

At the beginning of the game, users enter two names – their own and someone they are interested in.

This could be:

  • Close Friend
  • Stranger
  • Classmate
  • Fictional Character
  • Celebrities

Every time the player enters new data, Love Tester runs an analysis. It uses mysterious algorithms, and the result appears on the screen – percentage. But the game does not end there! This challenge also offers small comments that can range from humorous to surprisingly accurate and touching. Sometimes the machine gives unexpected hints, hinting that true love may be closer than it seems.

Artificial Intelligence

The plot of the game begins to develop further when you realize that it interacts not only with names, but also with real-life decisions. The more time you spend here, the more strange coincidences start to happen in your everyday life. People whose names you entered into Love Tester start to:

  • Show Attention to You
  • Unexpected Meetings Occur
  • Random Conversations Are No Longer Random

As our guests conduct more tests, they begin to notice that Love Tester does not just give out random wording. Over time, connections between certain names and comments become apparent, as if there is a riddle hidden in it that needs to be solved. In Love Tester game you can open different scenarios and ways of developing storylines. If you don’t believe this, then you should definitely check it out. Are you ready to get started and be surprised?
